Security Division

Security presence is part of the brand experience. Security members represent the E’Shae Speaks brand while on duty. Professionalism, respect, and positivity are mandatory.

Client Security Outsourcing Agreement

  • Services: presence, crowd flow control, guest management, designated positioning, and maintaining a safe professional environment.
  • Payment: payment is due at the start of the shift(s) (no payment will be received after service is complete).
  • Representation: security represents E’Shae Speaks while on duty.
  • Exclusivity: all bookings must be made directly through E’Shae Speaks (no side dealings).
  • Termination & Rights: services may be discontinued at any time for disrespect, misconduct, or negative impact.

Internal Team Pay Structure

  • 45 minutes: $100 (first 45 minutes).
  • 2+ hours (special/structured): can increase up to $1,000 depending on scope.
  • Volunteer Rotation: if a member works a paid event one week, the next event week is volunteer — mandatory rotation to keep the program sustainable.
  • Tips: event tips are split among active guards that night to keep it fair.
  • Team Standard: all team members must wear their custom tag at all E’Shae events.

First point of contact for concerns may be listed by the team lead (if applicable) inside your private internal docs. Public site remains client-facing and professional.

Booking Terms & Policies

Special Event Notice

The L$ 2,500 flat rate applies to standard hosting sets only. Custom pricing applies to baby showers, bachelor/bachelorette parties, adult venues/strip clubs, private/themed specialty events, and events without tip jar structure.

Co-Hosting Policy

E’Shae Productions is a solo-host brand. If an additional host is requested: L$ 500 Co-Host Collaboration Fee. Host must be disclosed in advance. Approval required prior to confirmation.

Booking Terms

  • 50% non-refundable deposit secures your date.
  • Remaining balance due prior to event start.
  • Please allow 24–48 hours for event write-ups/media deliverables after the event (when included).

Cancellation & Rescheduling

  • Deposit is non-refundable.
  • Cancellations 72+ hours before the event may be rescheduled once within 30 days (subject to availability).
  • If a new date is not secured within 30 days, the deposit is forfeited.
  • Cancellations within 72 hours forfeit the deposit.
  • Emergencies may be reviewed at discretion.

E’Shae Speaks reserves the right to refuse services to anyone or discontinue service with anyone, including members on the E’Shae team or production team, respectfully and at discretion.
